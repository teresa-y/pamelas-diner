What's Everyone Saying?

Five stars all around! Reviews from TripAdvisor, Google, and Yelp

"Pamela's Diner has been a staple of the Strip District and other neighborhoods throughout the city. They have the most incredible lyonnaise potatoes, hot cakes, breakfast varieties for all. I highly recommend this Strip District location just for the historical photographs and ambience of the 50's style diner." - Racheallee L.

"We came here on recommendation and we were not let down. I got the Pittsburgh hash - which was kielbasa, sauerkraut, and potatoes with choice of egg and toast. This hash was different and full of flavor. We also got an order of chocolate chip banana hot cakes. The hot cakes were like fried crepes - super thin and buttery and a little crunchy in parts. Filled with banana and chocolate chips... I could eat these all day for the rest of my life. So good. SO GOOD." - Erin H.

"I got the meat & eggs. Got bacon, wheat toast, eggs and their delicious potatoes! They had an amazing flavor! I usually dislike onions but they made it in a way where it was delicious. They give you a wonderful portion and it came out fast! Loved the butter on the toast, they put just the right amount. The bf got the tex mex omelet and loved it. The unsweetened tea was great too. It's definitely a local favorite and it was busy! Our waitress was amazing and made our day. We will definitely return!" - Valerie M.
